Frequently Asked Questions 💧❓💡

Find answers to common questions about our Freshmo products! 🤔🌟

What are Freshmo products made of? 🌿🔬

Our products are crafted with natural, eco-friendly ingredients to ensure freshness and sustainability. Our mouthwash is alcohol-free and formulated for antibacterial action. 🌱🚫🦠

How do I use Freshmo Mouthwash Sachets? 💧🎒

Simply tear open a sachet, pour into your mouth, swish for 30 seconds, and spit out. Perfect for on-the-go freshness! 🚶‍♀️💨

Do you offer bulk discounts? 💰📦

Contact us for bulk order inquiries, and we’ll provide tailored pricing options. 🤝📊

Is Freshmo mouthwash safe and tested? ✅🔬

Yes, our mouthwash is rigorously tested by the South African Bureau of Standards (SABS) and developed with scientific expertise from Professor David Katerere at Tshwane University of Technology. 🧪🎓

What is the shelf life of Freshmo mouthwash? 📅⏳

Our mouthwash has a two-year shelf life, ensuring long-lasting freshness and stability. 🌟🔒

Why choose Freshmo’s sachet packaging? 🎒✨

Our 10ml sachets are designed for portability and convenience, making oral hygiene easy for busy, on-the-go lifestyles. 🏃‍♂️💨
